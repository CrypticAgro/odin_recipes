Turkey Meatballs

Description

Ingredients

  • Ground Meat Turkey
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Add your ingredients into a mixing bowl
  2. Mix until desired consistency
  3. Create balls using mixture
  4. Broil in oven for 10 minutes
  5. Take out and enjoy
